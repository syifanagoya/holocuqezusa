Devon Edger Hughes (born August 1, 1972) is a professional wrestler from the United States. Hughes competed as D-Von Dudley for the World Wrestling Federation/Entertainment (WWF/E) from 1999 to 2005, and for Extreme Championship Wrestling (ECW) from 1995 to 199From 2005 until 2014, he was known as Brother Devon and Devon in Total Nonstop Action Wrestling (TNA). Hughes is one-half of the tag team Team 3D, along with his partner Mark LoMonaco (formerly known as The Dudley Boyz). Team 3D is one of the most successful tag teams in the history of professional wrestling, having won 23 world tag team titles and being the first tag team inducted into the TNA Hall of Fame. They are known for their unconventional ring attire and use of tables in their bouts. Hughes has held 25 major titles in TNA, WWE, ECW, and New Japan Pro Wrestling, including two TNA Television Championship reigns (NJPW).
