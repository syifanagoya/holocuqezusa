Adam Brody is a famous actor and producer from the United States. Adam Brody is widely known for his portrayal of Seth Cohen in the TV series, The O.C. Moreover, Brody has also appeared in films including Thank You for Smoking (2005), Mr. & Mrs. Smith (2005), Jennifer’s Body (2009), and In the Land of Women (2007).

Early Life and Childhood

Adam Brody was born in San Diego, California, United States on December 15, 1979. His birth name is Adam Jared Brody. He is 44 years old with the zodiac sign Sagittarius. Brody holds an American nationality and follows the Christian religion.

Regarding his family background, he is the son of Mark Alan Brody (Father) and Valerie Jill Bordy-Siefman (Mother). His mother, Valerie is a graphic designer whereas his father, Mark is an attorney by profession. He grew up along with his two siblings, Sean and Matthew.

Adam Brody – Education

Speaking of his educational background, He completed his secondary education at Scripps Ranch High School in America. He went to Wangenheim Middle School before that.

Later, he enrolled in a community college. But Brody left college early to focus on acting as a career.

Career and Professional Life

Talking about his career, Brody arrived in Hollywood at the age of 19 after leaving San Diego to pursue a career in acting. After that, he attended numerous auditions and acting classes for a full year.

He eventually began to get parts in TV shows and movies. Random Acts of Violence, Undressed, and American Pie 2 are a few of his early films.

Moreover, In 2003, he played Dustin Knight in the film Grind. He debuted in the television sitcom The O.C. that same year.

Caption: Adam Brody in the TV series, The O.C source: GQ.

His first career milestone came from his portrayal of Seth Cohen in the television series. He made appearances in the series till 2009. He interspersed important films including Death in Love, Smiley Face, and Mr. & Mrs. Smith.

Afterwards, in 2011, Brody provided the voice of Woodie in the television series Good Vibes. He had hits like Ready or Not, Yoga Hosers, and Welcome to the Jungle as each year goes by.

He last appeared in the suspense drama Promising Young Woman in 2020 as Jez. In addition to performing, he is involved in production work on the TV show StartUp and the short film Home Security.

Net Worth 2024

Adam Brody has earned a huge amount of money through his hard work and brilliance. His estimated net worth is around $16 million as of 2024. However, Brody has yet to be a part of brand endorsements.

Personal Life

Regarding his love life, Adam Brody is currently married to a famous American actress and singer, Leighton Meester.

The couple got together for the first time in March 2010 while filming The Oranges in Westchester, New York. They began to form a strong friendship while they were filming. They later began dating one another.

Caption: Adam Brody with Leighton Meester source: People.

In November 2013, the couple eventually got engaged. Afterwards, they were married in a very intimate ceremony on February 15, 2014.

Further, Arlo Day Brody, a daughter born in Whittier, California on August 4, 2015, is a blessing for the couple.

Adam Brody – Body Measurements

Adam Brody is handsome undoubtedly. He has a perfect height of 5 feet 11 inches (180 cm/1.80 m) and weighs around 74 kg (163 lbs). In addition, Brody has a hazel pair of eyes and dark brown hair colour.
