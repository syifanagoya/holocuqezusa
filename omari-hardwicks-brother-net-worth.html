All About Omari Hardwicks Personal Life &amp; More

Losing someone close to you is not easy and when it’s family members then the pain live forever. Similar happened to Omari Hardwick when he lost a son and two siblings in a span of three years.

Let us know more about Omari Hardwick’s family life tragedy along with the family struggles of interracial relationship. 

American actor Omari Hardwick is popular for the character portrayal of Vanderohe in Zack Snyder’s Army of the Dead in 2021. He has been in the entertainment showbiz for more than 20 years since 2001. 

Omari Hardwick Personal life: Wife & Interracial Relationship

The 48-year-old actor and his wife Jennifer Pfautch have been into beautiful matrimony for almost a decade since June 2012. However, it was a tough time for the actor to marry Jennifer citing her family not agreeing with the relationship as it was interracial. 

Nevertheless, the two stood up for their love. His wife is a strong advocate and supporter of racial equity and harmony. Talking about family, the pair’s first child was stillborn. After a year of marriage, they welcomed a baby daughter, Nova, age 8 in 2013.


Two years later, the family became four upon the arrival of baby son, Brave, age 5 in 2015.

What Is Omari Hardwick’s Net Worth?

Omari’s net worth is over $5 million from professional acting since 2001. Some of his movies are Army of the Dead (2021), Shot Caller (2017), Reach Me (2014), The A-Team (2010), and Beauty Shop (2005). 

Besides movies, his TV work includes Army of the Dead: Lost Vegas (2022), Chase (2010), and Saved (2006).

Omari Hardwick Bio & Family: Wiki, Height, Brother

The actor was born Omari Latif Hardwick on 9 January 1974 in Savannah, Georgia. Talking about his parents, he was born to Joyce and Clifford Hardwick III. He has a brother Jamil Hardwick. Sadly one of his brothers Malik and a sister Shani Hardwick passed away at the age of 45 and 33.

Omari went to Marist School in Brookhaven, Georgia per wiki. The actor is 5 feet 10 inches (1.78 m) tall in height. 

Some Interesting Facts About Omari Hardwick

  • Omari’s music videos are First Began (2008), Flipmode (2017), Break My Heart (2011) and No More (2000). 
  • The actor declared himself at the NFL Draft but was not selected.
  • During his early days, he did poetry and minored in theater. 
  • His father is an attorney.
