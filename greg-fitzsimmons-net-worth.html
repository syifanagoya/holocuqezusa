The Road to Success

Greg Fitzsimmons’ journey in the entertainment industry is a classic tale of talent meeting perseverance.

Early Beginnings

Greg’s career trajectory took its first significant leap at Boston University. Here, not only did he earn his undergraduate degree, but he also honed his craft in stand-up comedy, performing with the comedy troupe Uncontrolled Substance.

This experience was pivotal, setting the stage for his late-night debut on the Late Show with David Letterman in 1996.
