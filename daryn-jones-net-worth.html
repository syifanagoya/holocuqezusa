The Journey Begins

Born on a chilly January day in 1978, in Victoria, British Columbia, Daryn Jones embarked on a journey that would see him become one of Canada’s most beloved comedians and TV personalities.

His career kicked off in the stand-up comedy clubs at the tender age of 17, a testament to his early passion and talent for making people laugh.

This early start laid the foundation for a career that would span various facets of entertainment, from sketch comedy to hosting national TV shows.

Rise to Fame

Jones’s big break came when he and Morgan Mista Mo Smith brought their sketch comedy show Buzz from the Toronto Community Access Channel to the Comedy Network in 2000.

A Diverse Career

Jones’s versatility saw him transition smoothly from sketch comedy to hosting. He became a familiar face on YTV’s The Zone, and later, as the host of MTV Canada’s flagship series MTV Live.

Beyond Television

Jones’s influence extended beyond the television screen. He returned to radio, hosting shows on KISS 92 FM and later on KISS 92.5, proving his versatility across media platforms.
