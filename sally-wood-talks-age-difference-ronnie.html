Sally Wood wishes age gap between her and Ronnie 'wasn't there'

 Posted on February 1, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  260 words  |  Aldo Pusey

    Sally Humphreys, 36, has revealed that she wishes there wasn't an age gap between her and her husband Ronnie Wood, 67.Speaking to , the theatre producer said: "I know it's there. And I wish it wasn't, but it is. 

    Sally and Ronnie Wood married in 2012

    "At no point, years ago, did I say to myself: 'I think I'll go out with someone twice my age', but that is what has happened. "The couple had been friends for years but began going out in 2011. They were married in a brief ceremony which took place on 21 December 2012 on the eighth-floor penthouse suite of the Dorchester hotel in central London.Around 45 guests gathered for an intimate ceremony including Ronnie's three children Leah, Jesse and Tyrone and several celebrity friends. Among them were Jesse's wife, Radio 1 DJ Fearne Cotton and Stereophonics singer Kelly Jones. Best men Rod Stewart and Sir Paul McCartney attended with their glam other halves Penny Lancaster and Nancy Shevell.

    Sally says Ronnie is "funny, kind and affectionate"

    During the interview, Sally is full of praise for her husband of nearly two years. "He's a very nice and caring person.

    She continues: "He's very funny, kind and affectionate and welcoming. "The couple have a lot in common, mainly their mutual love for murder mysteries. "We love things like and ," says Sally.
